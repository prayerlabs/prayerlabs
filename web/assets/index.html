Pray

Follow the people and causes you care about. Get their freshly squeezed prayer updates and requests. Let them know you’ve prayed for them by clicking the lightning symbol (like a “like”) or message them with an encouragement. Schedule when and how often you want to pray for them. Get prayed for in the same way.

Grow

Read inspiring daily reading plans on subjects ranging from bible books to love and relationships to coping with stress. Submit plans of your own to bless the community. Pastors turn your sermons into mini devotional series.

Give

Discover life changing causes & ministries. See the charities your friends are supporting. Get their prayer updates. Make easy in app gifts to them. Worship is at the heart of prayer and generosity is at the heart of worship. Every day new causes will be available to give you new opportunities to support God’s work around the world.

Circles

Arrange your friends into Circles so that you can make your prayer updates relevant to the right people. More private prayers to your close friends Circle. More public prayers to you small group or church mates circle.

Groups

Groups are private spaces for each member to share prayers and updates with each other. You might make a group for your accountability partners, a men’s or women’s discipleship group or the church leadership group.

Pages

Pages are public spaces for causes and ministries to post updates about their work and to get prayer support and in app giving. You can find out what’s on the heart of your local church or an international aid organisation and schedule how often and when they appear in your daily prayer feed.
